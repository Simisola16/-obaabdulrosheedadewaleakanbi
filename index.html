About The Oluwo

His Royal Majesty, Oba Abdulrosheed Adewale Akanbi

Oluwo of Iwo Land is the paramount ruler of Iwo Kingdom, a first class natural ruler in Yorubaland. His Royal Majesty, Oba Abdulrosheed Adewale Akanbi, ascended the throne on November 19, 2015 as the 16th Oluwo of Iwo Land.

Oba Akanbi is known for his progressive leadership, commitment to the development of Iwo Land, and his efforts to promote Yoruba culture and traditions globally. He is a visionary leader dedicated to fostering unity and progress in his domain.

Under his reign, Iwo Land has witnessed significant development in infrastructure, education, and cultural renaissance. The Oluwo is also recognized for his advocacy for peace and his initiatives to empower the youth and women in his kingdom.
