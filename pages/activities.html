Recent Activities

Annual Cultural Festival

The Annual Cultural Festival was held from August 15-18, 2023 at the Royal Palace, celebrating Yoruba heritage with traditional music, dance, and ceremonies.

Youth Empowerment Program

A youth empowerment program was launched in May 2023 to provide skills training and educational scholarships to young people in Iwo Land.

Community Development Visit

His Royal Majesty conducted a community development visit in January 2023, engaging with local artisans and leaders to promote economic growth.

Diplomatic Meeting

In March 2023, the Oluwo hosted a diplomatic meeting to strengthen international partnerships and cultural exchanges.
